Wind Speed

There appears to be no correlation between wind speed and proximity to the equator. At all latitudes (-60 to 70 latitude), most cities experienced a range of wind speeds from (0 to 15 mph), with two outliners near 60 degrees latitude that experience 35 mph winds all the time.
